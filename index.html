Overview People Sponsors RECON

Mapping epidemics

This mini-hackfest will be dedicated to mapping outbreak data and visualising associated spatial processes. Things we will look into include:

  • ⚫ mapping incidence data using incidence objects.
  • ⚫ visualising contact data in the geographic space
  • ⚫ predicting and mapping spatial diffusion of cases based on passenger flow data
  • ⚫ ... and probably more!
